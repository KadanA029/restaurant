Toni's Fine Italian Dining

Our Story

I was born and raised in Tuscany, a hilly small town known for its serenity and its vineyards. Everyday as a kid, I used to always join my grandma in the kitchen when she would create delicious recipes for our family every evening. As I grew older, I slowly started being more ambivalent, and being able to contribute more. On my 12th Birthday, I staarted becoming the main chef in the house, producing masterpieces in the kitchen to feed my family for dinner. I would always feel so satisfied seeing the people around me enjoy delicious foods. When I moved to the U.S, I knew I wanted to continue fullfilling my passion of making those around me happy through delicious food creations. I then started Toni's Fine Italian Dining and was fortunate enough to grow it to its current state. Today, our mission is clear. It is a continuation of my past joy in creating food, and a continuation of my desire to gift those the pleasure of tasting delicious food crafts.
